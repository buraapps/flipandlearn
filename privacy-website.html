Flip & Learn

Website Privacy Notice  ·  Last updated: May 1, 2026

The short version: The flipandlearn.app website collects your email address only if you sign up for the Android-launch waiting list. We send you one email when Android is ready, then we delete the list. Nothing else.

Why this notice exists

This notice covers the flipandlearn.app website — specifically, the form on the home page where you can sign up to be notified when the Android version of Flip & Learn launches.

For privacy details about the Flip & Learn iOS/Android app itself, see our app privacy policy instead.

What we collect

When you submit the Android-launch sign-up form, we receive:

  • The email address you typed in.
  • Your browser language (e.g., "de", "fr") — so we can send the launch email in the right language.
  • Your browser user-agent string — only used to debug delivery problems if anything goes wrong.

That is the entire dataset. We don't track you across pages, we don't use cookies for analytics, and we don't store your IP address.

Why we collect it

One single purpose: to send you a single email when the Android version of Flip & Learn becomes available on Google Play.

We don't add you to a newsletter, we don't send marketing, and we don't use your email for anything else.

Where it's stored

The list lives in a private Google Sheet that only the Flip & Learn developer can access. Google acts as a data processor here; the data sits on Google Workspace servers, which are EU-compliant.

The list is never sold, never shared with advertisers, and never made available to anyone outside Flip & Learn.

How long we keep it

Until the Android launch email has been sent, plus a short window in case of bounces. After that — within 30 days of the launch announcement — the entire list is deleted.

If you submit your email and then change your mind before the launch email goes out, you can ask us to delete it sooner (see below).

Your rights

You can ask us at any time to:

  • Tell you what email address (if any) we have on file for you.
  • Delete your email from the list immediately.
  • Withdraw your consent for being contacted (effectively the same as deletion).

The legal basis for collecting your email is your consent — the act of submitting the form. You can withdraw that consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, just write to support@flipandlearn.app.

If you live in the EU, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In Germany (where Flip & Learn is based), that's the Bundesbeauftragte für den Datenschutz or your state-level equivalent.

Analytics

This site uses Cloudflare Web Analytics, a cookieless analytics service that counts page views, referrers, and country information. No cookies are set, no personal data is collected, and no individual visitors are tracked across sessions.

Changes to this notice

If we change how the website collects data, we'll update this page and bump the "Last updated" date at the top.

Contact

Questions about this notice? Email support@flipandlearn.app — we usually reply within 48 hours.
