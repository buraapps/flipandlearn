Everything kids love, everything parents trust

🃏

Classic Memory Game

Flip cards to find matching pairs. Beautiful 3D flip animations make every match satisfying.

🔊

Speaks Every Word

When a match is found, the app speaks the word out loud in the chosen language using native text-to-speech.

⭐

Stars & Trophies

Earn 1-3 stars per game. Collect stars to unlock 9 different card back designs including the legendary Dino at 1000 stars.

🧒

Personal Avatar

Children choose their own animal avatar and name. The app greets them personally and celebrates their wins.

📅

Word of the Day

A new featured word every day with pronunciation. A fun reason to open the app daily.

🎃

Seasonal Themes

The app automatically decorates itself for Easter, Halloween, Christmas and all four seasons. Kids notice and get excited!
